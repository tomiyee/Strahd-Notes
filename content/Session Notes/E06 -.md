> [!example] Vallaki - Day XX

We arrived at the gates of [[Vallaki]] only to discover that the guards were suspicious of silver. They began searching our belongings. [[Ireena]] tried to hide her silver bolts but failed her sleight of hand check. [[Evelyn]] managed to hide hers successfully, while [[Risodo]] took a more creative approach—burying his bolts in a nearby bush, under a pile of filth, for maximum camouflage.  

An imposing man appeared and instructed the guards to change the town’s proclamations. The new notice declared: *"The celebration of the Blazing Sun — attendance and children required."* This man was later identified as [[Isek Strazni]], a big, burly figure who reached out toward [[Ireena]] in a way that suggested recognition. [[Risodo]] noticed this and quickly ushered everyone toward the tavern.  

Inside, we found the innkeeper and his wife, a man with a scar, a woman sitting at the bar, a colorfully dressed half-elf lounging in a booth, and two sulky men sulking in the corner. We also learned that [[Vallaki]] deals exclusively in electrum coins, not silver.

> [!important] Item: - Silver Coins  
> [!important] Item: - Silver Bolts (Ireena)  
> [!important] Item: - Silver Bolts (Risodo — buried in bush)

We spoke to the shopkeeper and unlocked a new side quest: travel to the [[Wizard of Wines]] vineyard to investigate why shipments of Purple Grape Mash #3 had stopped.  

We also met [[Rictavio]]—or "Tav"—a bard who keeps a monkey named Picolo with the local toymaker [[Blinsky]]. He feeds the monkey every day. This prompted [[Evelyn]] to share that she once ran a “miniature” zoo, complete with monkeys, parrots, a macaw, and even belugas.  

[[Rictavio]] told us a story about Count Belasco, a rather vain nobleman who wore a wig.  

Later, the brothers [[Karl]] and [[Nikolai Wachter]], sons of [[Lady Fiona Wachter]] and opponents of the [[Baron]], treated Deathcheater to some wine. They drank themselves into a stupor and ended up passed out in a shed nearby.

We ended the day by going to bed and reaching level 4.
