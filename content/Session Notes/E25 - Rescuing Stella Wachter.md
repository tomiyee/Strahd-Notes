> [!example] [[Vallaki]] - Day ??

Now that we have the Heartstone, the party returns to the town of Vallaki with [[Lady Fiona Wachter]] and [[Victor Vallakovich]]. [[Victor Vallakovich]] explains that in order to conduct the ritual, we need to find a Ley Line. [[Lady Fiona Wachter]] tells us there is a Ley Line that goes through the basement of her house. 

> [!example] Basement of the [[Wachter]] household - Day ??

The party takes a Short Rest once we return to the [[Wachter]] household. In order to do the ritual, [[Lady Fiona Wachter]] once again disables the protection over her house. 

In the basement, [[Lady Fiona Wachter]] tells us about how as a child, she was told the basement had a monster. Then, once she inherited the estate, she found that the basement was empty. There was no evidence of life down there aside from the scratches on the wall. (Foreshadowing...)

[[Victor Vallakovich]] begins the ritual. He begins to chant, reading from the tome. The Heartstone hums. Suddenly a dark shadow darts around the room. The clawed shadow vanishes as the magic surges with the conclusion of the spell. We feel a lurching and are in the Ethereal Plane.

In the Ethereal plane, [[Stella Wachter]] appears before us. However, she is just a specter. [[Victor Vallakovich]] remarks that [[Stella Wachter]] is losing her tangibility faster than he expected. She's already beginning to fade from the waist down. 

The party hears a laughter in the distance and before us manifests [[Erasmus van Richten]], a ghostly figure that appears no more than 14 years old. He followed his father into Barovia. Doctor Rudolph van Richten, what he thinks is a year ago, but even [[Lady Fiona Wachter]] doesn't recognize the name.  He appears slightly crestfallen when the party says we're here to bring [[Stella Wachter]] back to the Material Plane. 

Does he know what the inscriptions on the wall are? He may know what caused them. "He's not someone we... want to meet." Viktor announces that he's finished the summoning circle and tells Stella to go into the center. During that ritual, it will attract evil spirits trying to take her place. Our job is to prevent them. 

> [!important] New House Rule: We can cast two leveled spells in a turn ^dcabdc


We fight 4 specters. More approach once those are defeated but they hear a shrieking in the distance and they leave. A figure begins to coalese in the air above. A being of bone and robes. Dark vacant eye sockets. It's skeletal claw tipped hands, the same as the figure that appeared as we entered. "I am [[Leo Dilisnya]]. We come for the blood that is owed." It reaches out pointing at [[Stella Wachter]]. 

He gives a lore dump on Strahd and why the Wachter family line are "traitors."

> [!cite] [[Leo Dilisnya]]'s lore claims
> - In life, [[Leo Dilisnya]] was a guard at [[Castle Ravenloft]].
> - There was a wedding there between [[Sergei von Zarovich]] and [[Tatiana]]
> - [[Strahd von Zarovich]] kills [[Sergei von Zarovich]] and pursue his bride [[Tatiana]]
> - [[Leo Dilisnya]] is given the order to avenge [[Sergei von Zarovich]] by killing Strahd.
> - Later, when [[Strahd von Zarovich]] became a vampire, he slaughtered the castle guards
> - [[Leo Dilisnya]] managed to escape and hid in [[Abbey of Saint Markovia]].
> - His sister, [[Lovina Wachter]] (maiden name Dilisnya), had her personal body guards assist in the bloodshed.
> - Strahd captures him and turns [[Leo Dilisnya]] into a vampire spawn.
> - [[Leo Dilisnya]] is banished to the Ethereal Plane to have the bloodthirst make him insane over hundreds of years. 

He believes that [[Lovina Wachter]] was corrupted by her Wachter husband made her a traitor. He is determined to make the [[Wachter]] family line perish. 

[[Deathcheater]]'s insight tells her that [[Leo Dilisnya]] is telling a half truth. 

> [!cite] The lore dump ver batem
> > In life, [[Leo Dilisnya]] was an assassin of the [[Ba’al Verzi]] who betrayed his order’s vows when he sought to kill Strahd in vengeance for the death of his brother, [[Reinhold Dilisnya]], who Strahd knowingly sent to die to win a battle against the [[Order of the Silver Dragon]]. On the morning of [[Sergei von Zarovich]]’s wedding, Leo climbed to the window of Strahd’s royal chambers, carrying with him a cursed [[Ba’al Verzi]] dagger that prevented its victims from being resurrected. When [[Lovina Wachter]]’s bodyguards engaged the castle guards to defend Strahd, it was Leo who gave the order to have the Wachters themselves killed, furious that his sister had elected to “betray” him and [[Reinhold Dilisnya]]’s memory.

[[Leo Dilisnya]] cannot be reasoned with, so we conduct battle. When [[Leo Dilisnya]] is defeated. he drops an Ancient tome bound by leather.

We return to the ritual as [[Victor Vallakovich]] is wrapping up. Before the party leaves, Erasmus bestows upon us two useful facts. 
- He's noticed spiders passing in and out of the Ethereal Plane in a settlement to the southwest. 
- He's noticed Strahd sometimes enters the Ethereal Plane atop his flaming horse. He doesn't know what he does in the Ethereal plane. 

Back in the Material Plane, [[Lady Fiona Wachter]] gifts us an oil lantern. We assume it is magical but have yet to identify it. 

[[Victor Vallakovich]] gives us the Spirit Mirror to peer into the Ethereal Plane, into a distance of 60 ft. It can be any size, from hand size to full size.
