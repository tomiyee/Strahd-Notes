> [!example] Village of Barovia - Day 1 Morning

The party of [[Evelyne du Westhaven]], [[Deathcheater]], [[Boomer]], and [[Risodo]] arrive in the land of Barovia. We encounter a dead commoner that failed to post the Burgomaster [[Ismark the Greater]]'s warning to stop people from entering Barovia. 

[[Risodo]] gives him a humble wedding using one of his vials of holy water to prevent his rising as an undead. [[Deathcheater]] suggested a "Goliath burial" which involved taking the corpse to a mountain and leaving him to the vultures.

> [!important] Item: -1 Holy Water

As we enter the village, we find [[Morgantha|an old lady]] selling *Dream Pies*. [[Risodo]], [[Boomer]], and [[Deathcheater]] each get a Dream Pie. Only Deathcheater resists the sleepy side effects.

We stay at [[Blood on the Vine Inn]]. Before bed, [[Evelyne du Westhaven]] tries to buy a Dream Pastry because the inn has no food. Outside, she witnesses a little boy get thrown to the Dream Pastry lady. 

> [!example] Village of Barovia - Day 2 Morning

The next morning, we meet the burgomaster, [[Ismark the Lesser]], son of [[Ismark the Greater]]. His father is dead, and he needs his sister, [[Ireena Kolyana]] escorted to a neighboring town for safety ([[Strahd von Zarovich]] wants her). However, she won't leave until [[Ismark the Greater|her father]] gets a proper burial. 

They haven't been able to hold the funeral ceremony because the village priest won't leave the church. Risodo and Boomer go to investigate while Evelyne and [[Deathcheater]] go to investigate the house.

**The Church**
The priest won't leave because his son has been turned into a vampire and locked into the basement screaming in hunger pains. Risodo feeds blood to the son, taking damage and losing maximum health. The son stops screaming in pain and the priest agrees to host the funeral.

> [!important] Inspiration +1

**The House Visited by the Dream Pie**
[[Evelyne du Westhaven]] and [[Deathcheater]] break into the house. They find the mother hanging herself with a note:

> My dear Lucian, I could not protect you from that wretched old woman. I should have done more. […] Taken up to that wretched windmill.

They chose not to tell [[Boomer]] or [[Risodo]].

> [!example] Village of Barovia - Day 2 Afternoon

Everyone collects at the church where the priest tells us a story. A wizard said they could fight [[Strahd von Zarovich]] and organized a revolt. [[Strahd von Zarovich]] killed the wizard and turned the priest's son into a vampire.
