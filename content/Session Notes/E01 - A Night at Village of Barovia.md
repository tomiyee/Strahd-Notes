# Arriving in Barovia
> [!example] Village of Barovia - Day 1 Morning
- The party of [[Evelyne du Westhaven]], [[Deathcheater]], [[Boomer]], and [[Risodo]] arrive in the land of Barovia. 
- We find a dead commoner that died trying to post the Burgomaster [[Ismark the Greater]]'s warning to stop people from entering Barovia. 
- [[Risodo]] gives a humble burial using a vial of holy water to prevent undead. 
- [[Deathcheater]] suggested a "Goliath burial," which involved taking the corpse to a mountain to leave them to the vultures. 
> [!important] Item: -1 Holy Water
- As we enter [[Vallaki]], we find [[Morgantha|an old lady]] selling *Dream Pies*. [[Risodo]], [[Boomer]], and [[Deathcheater]] each get a Dream Pie. Only Deathcheater resists the sleepy side effects.
- We stay at [[Blood on the Vine Inn]]. 
- Before bed, [[Evelyne du Westhaven]] tries to buy a Dream Pastry because the inn has no food. Outside, she witnesses a little boy get thrown to the Dream Pastry lady. 
# Speaking to [[Ismark the Lesser]] for Escort Mission
> [!example] Village of Barovia - Day 2 Morning
- The next morning, we meet the burgomaster, [[Ismark the Lesser]], son of [[Ismark the Greater]]. His father is dead, and he needs his sister, [[Ireena Kolyana]] escorted to a neighboring town for safety ([[Strahd von Zarovich]] wants her). 
- However, she won't leave until [[Ismark the Greater|her father]] gets a proper burial. They haven't been able to hold the funeral ceremony because the priest, Father [[Donavich]] won't leave the church. 
- [[Risodo]] and [[Boomer]] go to investigate while [[Evelyne du Westhaven]] and [[Deathcheater]] go to investigate the house [[Evelyne du Westhaven]] saw last night.
## Plot 1 - The Church
- Father [[Donavich]] won't leave because his son has been turned into a vampire and locked into the basement screaming in hunger pains. 
- Risodo feeds blood to the son, taking damage and losing maximum health. 
- The son stops screaming in pain and the priest agrees to host the funeral.
> [!important] Inspiration +1
## Plot 2 - Dream Pie House
- [[Evelyne du Westhaven]] and [[Deathcheater]] break into the house. They find the mother dead, hanging herself, with a note:
> My dear Lucian, I could not protect you from that wretched old woman. I should have done more. […] Taken up to that wretched windmill.
- They chose not to tell [[Boomer]] or [[Risodo]].
