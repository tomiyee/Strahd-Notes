Requires attunement
Acquired in [[E25 - Rescuing Stella Wachter]]
We need to spend 4 Hours of dedicated reading time and succeed a check to decipher a book. 

Unlocked as part of experiencing the interactive chapters of the tome, the person attuned to the Tome can now cast, once per day:
- *Illusory script* as of [[E25 - Rescuing Stella Wachter]]
- *Unseen Servant* as of [[E30 - Travel to Krezk]]
- *Gentle Repose* as of [[E34 - Strahd becomes the land]]


You can take things out of the book. 

Damage taken in the book are still damage in real world.
